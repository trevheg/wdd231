Site Name: D&D Free Rules Reference

This name was chosen because this site will be a reference page for the free rules of D&D

Site Purpose

This site will provide reference tools for people interested in various aspects of D&D, such as races, classes, monsters, and equipment.

Scenarios

  • What are the various classes and races I can play and what are their abilities?
  • What are the differences in stats for various weapons and armor?
  • What monsters are available in the D&D free rules and what are their abilities?

Typography

I will use the Roboto font because it is a good looking font that is easy to read. It is also used in various D&D websites such as dndbeyond.
